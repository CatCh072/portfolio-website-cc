Olive

Don't forget your health!


Olive is a responsive health and wellbeing portal created for health-conscious individuals that are on the go, that juggle many things, that are just forgetful or all of these things.

Olive helps you develop better habits by creating personalized acitivity reminders, medication alerts, and logs to keep track of good habits. You can also keep track of bad habits and possible triggers.

The Challenge

The challenge was to create a responsive portal for our health-conscious users that wish to stay on top of their health and wellness for greater balance in their busy lives. They need a way to access, enter and share their health information. They also need a way to form more healthy habits and get rid of bad ones. We will know this to be true when we see our users using our app to achieve their health goals and make less inquires to their doctors.

Iterations

Accessibility

Olive was created for a variety of users that would like to improve their health. During the early stages of development, accessibility was always an important factor. I have tried my best to keep ample signifiers and the color theme simple and clean as possible but there is always room for improvement.

Iterations

The Goal

Olive, a responsive health reminder portal was created to let users personalize the portal to their specific needs. Users will be able to create personal alerts to build healthy habits for themseleves and their dependent/s. Users will also be able to share their progress with their doctor or trainer and recieve the latest health information.

What I have learned

  • Not everyone is the same. Everyone is created differently and requires different needs.
  • An interface with too much information or not organized icons can lead to user confusion and frustration.
  • Personal information is always a target for hackers. We must find a way to access, input, store, and share information securely.
  • Testing and empathy is essential for my product to grow and evolve to a better product.

Future Iterations

A feature to input information (for reminders) in quicker and/or easier way would be helpful for those that constantly have new reminders. Health care professionals, trainers, etc. could to send their advised routine/regimens directly to their patient/client and the user will have the option to accept and edit as needed.

UI for Olive will have to continuously grow. Keeping in mind all the different users, Olive needs to be clean and easy to read for our more mature users but also has to be up to date (design trends that are accessibile for everyone) and interesting for our younger audience.
